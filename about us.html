our story

welcome to food mania,

At Food Mania cafe and resto, we blend comfort and creativity to bring a dining experience like no other. Founded with a passion for food and love for community. Our space is a cozy escape where flavors from around the world meet a warm, local touch.

Whether you're here for a fresh-brewed coffee, a hearty brunch, or a candlelit dinner, every dish is crafted with care, using the freshest ingredients. Our chefs bring innovation to the table while staying true to the soul of traditional cooking. Come enjoy delicious meals, relaxed vibes, and moments that feel like home.
